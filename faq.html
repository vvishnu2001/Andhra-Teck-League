Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the name of the event, and what is its purpose?

Andhra Teck League. The purpose of the Andhra Teck League (ATL) is to encourage creativity, skill development, and a passion for technical advancement in students by inspiring, educating, and empowering them in the field of emerging technologies.

2.What are the stages of the event?

There are 3 Levels,
Preliminary level will be done online after registering to respective competitions. Zonal level will be done at 5 zones (5 various districts) of the Andhra Pradesh.
Final level will take place at Vishakhapatnam (Andhra University North Campus).

3.Who can attend? Same college students or can merge with any other colleges?

Only same college / school students should attend and it is mandatory for everyone to show their Identity cards at the events to get entry.

4.How can I register for the event?

Visit registration page in our official website. If you are school students, register in junior section. If you are college students, register in senior section.

5.Is there an entry fee for the event?

There is no entry fee for Preliminary level. The preliminary level is open for all the students. Participants who are into Zonal level should pay an entry fee.

6.What is the registration fee and will differ for the medium like Junior Level and Senior Level?

Yes, there will be registration fee for Zonal level, which is 349/- for Junior Level and 499/- for Senior Level.

7.What payment methods are accepted for registration?

Only online payments will be accepted. The details will be shared in the above-mentioned website.

8.What activities are planned for the event?

There are 6 technical competitions we are conducting all those are mentioned in the ‘Competitions’ page in the website. Spot events will also be conducted.

9.Can I get a refund if I am unable to attend the event?

No refund will be processed if you fail to attend any of Zonal level or Final level event.

10.What are the accommodation arrangements?

For any accommodation assistance, please contact to the support numbers mentioned in the website and Exclusive accommodations are not available.

11.When can I register?

Exclusive dates are available in the website, for further information please go through the ‘Home’ page in the website.

12.Can I register at the venue?

No. There is no spot registration.

13.If I have questions, who can I contact?

For contact details please go through our website.

14.Can parents or guardians attend the event?

Yes. We encourage 1 to 2 members of your family to attend the event along with you.

15.Will there be any prizes or awards given out during the event?

Yes. By giving out awards, we show our appreciation for and recognition of the students' achievements.

16.What if a student interested to participate in 2 Events at a time? Will that be possible?

Can participate, but students should plan according to the time and the registration fee should be paid for both the events respectively.

17.Will there be any participation certificates for the students?

Yes, we provide a participation certificates for the students who will be selected for Final level event.

18.Will the problem statements same for all the standards?

No, the questions will be shuffled according to the level of participants.

Rules of Andhra Teck League (ATL):

1. Eligibility Criteria:

- Andhra Teck league is open to all the school students and Engineering graduates of Andhra Pradesh.

2. Registration:

- All participants must register through website of Andhra Teck League, to participate in the event.
Registrations should be completed before the specified deadline in the website.
Incomplete or late registrations may not be considered If considered fine is applicable.

3. Team Composition:

- Each team must consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.
All team members must belong to the same institution or organization.

4. Originality:

- Submissions and projects must be original and created by the participants themselves.
Plagiarism or the use of copyrighted materials is strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.

5. Contact Information:

- Each team should provide valid email IDs and contact numbers during registration.
The provided contact information should be accurate and up-to-date.
We are not responsible for the communication if the provided participant’s contact details are invalid.

6. Equipment Requirements:

- Each team is required to have a minimum of 2 laptops and other equipment for participating in respective competitions of the event.
Participants must bring their own laptops with all necessary software, tools installed and other equipment.

7. Event Communication:

- All official communication regarding the event will be sent to the registered email IDs provided by the teams.
Participants are responsible for checking their emails regularly for updates and announcements.

8. Code of conduct:

- Each and Every student should carry their respective educational Identity Card.
Participants must conduct themselves in a respectful and sportsmanlike manner throughout the event.
Any form of harassment, cheating, or unethical behavior will not be tolerated and may result in immediate disqualification.

9. Event Format:

- The specific format and rules for each competition will be provided to the registered teams before the event date.
Participants must adhere to the guidelines and requirements set by the event coordinators for each challenge.

10. Decision of Judges:

- The decision of the judging panel will be final and binding in all matters related to the event and its evaluation.

11. Safety Measures:

- Participants must be acknowledged to all safety guidelines provided by the organizers during the event.
Safety is of utmost importance, especially in events involving physical prototypes, flying drones and robotics.

12. Photography and Videography:

- Participants may be required to allow photography or videography of their projects for promotional purposes.

13. Liability:

- Participants are responsible for their own safety and well-being during the event.
The event organizers will not be liable for any injuries, damages, or losses incurred during the event.

14. Changes and Updates:

- Participants should regularly check the official website or communication channels for any changes, updates, or clarifications related to the event.
